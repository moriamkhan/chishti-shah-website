About the Firm

Built on
Principled Practice

Chishti & Shah is a Lahore-based firm of Advocates and Legal Consultants committed to providing legal services of the highest standard. Our practice brings together deep expertise in corporate law, commercial litigation, and public sector representation to serve clients across Pakistan's most demanding legal environments.

The firm's work philosophy centers on absolute commitment to clients' interests — thorough research and preparation, honest and pragmatic advice, and advocacy grounded in an in-depth knowledge of the law. Our lawyers are driven by excellence, professionalism, tolerance, and integrity — qualities passed down through the traditions of Pakistan's finest legal practitioners.

We combine global legal standards with local excellence, maintaining the quality necessary to meet our clients' needs in every engagement, whether advisory, transactional, or contentious.

Litigation

Our litigators appear before civil courts, the Lahore High Court, and superior courts across Pakistan. We handle a broad range of contentious matters — commercial disputes, writ petitions, property litigation, and constitutional challenges — with thoroughness and strategic clarity. Our approach is grounded in rigorous preparation, honest assessment, and effective advocacy.

Arbitration

We represent clients in domestic and international arbitration proceedings across commercial, construction, energy, and employment disputes. Our team is experienced in all major arbitral rules and advises on arbitration clause drafting, seat selection, enforcement, and challenge proceedings.

Advisory / Transactional

Our lawyers have extensive experience advising clients across all stages of complex commercial transactions — both domestic and cross-border. We advise on entity formation, due diligence, contractual obligations, foreign direct investment frameworks, regulatory compliance, and project structuring. We provide advice in the format most suited to the client — by telephone, in writing, or in person.

White-Collar Defence

One of the firm's signature strengths. We have represented individuals and institutions in high-stakes NAB, Federal Investigation Agency, Anti-Corruption Establishment, and financial crime proceedings. Our work includes designing litigation frameworks for asset recovery, managing parallel proceedings, and advising on anti-bribery and corruption compliance for corporates.

Consultancy & Legislative Drafting

We advise government bodies, regulatory authorities, and private organisations on legal reform, policy development, and legislative drafting. We assist in the preparation of subordinate legislation, regulatory frameworks, and institutional compliance structures aligned with best international standards.

Internal Investigations & Anti-Corruption

We conduct internal investigations for corporations facing allegations of fraud, financial misconduct, or regulatory breaches. Our team assists with investigation design, evidence preservation, employee interviews, and remediation — all handled with strict confidentiality and a focus on protecting organisational interests.

Business Tax Planning

We provide strategic tax advice to businesses, helping structure operations, transactions, and investments in a tax-efficient manner. Our advisory covers corporate taxation, withholding tax obligations, provincial levies, customs and duties, and dispute resolution before tax tribunals. We have experience before the Punjab Revenue Authority and with SECP regulatory filings.

Trademark & IP Services

We advise on the protection, registration, enforcement, and licensing of intellectual property rights — including trademarks, copyright, and trade dress. We represent clients in IP disputes before courts and tribunals, and assist in building IP portfolios and licensing frameworks that protect commercial value.

Secretarial Services

We provide company secretarial services including incorporation, filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP), maintenance of statutory registers, preparation of board resolutions and general meeting documentation, and annual compliance filings.

Employment & HR Advisory

We advise employers and employees on the full spectrum of employment matters — from agreement drafting and structuring of employment terms, to disciplinary procedures, redundancy, and contentious employment disputes. We have particular experience in logistics, education, and public sector employment contexts.

Banking & Finance

Advising financial institutions and borrowers on lending structures, security documentation, regulatory compliance, and dispute resolution. We have extensive experience with panel representation for major Pakistani banks.

Company Law

Advising on all aspects of corporate law including formation, governance, restructuring, directors' duties, minority shareholder rights, and SECP compliance and regulatory filings.

Commercial Law

Contract drafting, negotiation, and dispute resolution across the full spectrum of commercial transactions — from logistics and supply chain to financial services and construction.

Competition / Antitrust Law

Advising on merger control, anti-competitive practices, abuse of dominance, and CCPA compliance. Representing clients before the Competition Commission of Pakistan.

Constitutional & Administrative Law

Filing and defending writ petitions before the Lahore High Court and Supreme Court of Pakistan. Advising public authorities and private parties on administrative law challenges, judicial review, and constitutional rights.

Employment & Labour Law

Comprehensive employment law advice to employers and employees — agreement drafting, statutory compliance, disciplinary processes, and contentious employment tribunal proceedings.

Environmental Law

Advising developers, industrialists, and regulators on environmental compliance, permit applications, and environmental liability. Experience gained through representation in corporate and commercial matters involving environmental protection obligations.

Financial Crime

Fraud, money laundering, corruption, and regulatory investigations. We defend individuals and companies in proceedings before the FIA, NAB, and Anti-Corruption Establishment, and advise on preventive compliance frameworks.

Foreign Direct Investment

Advising foreign investors on Pakistan's regulatory and legal framework for FDI — from initial structuring and sectoral approvals through to joint ventures, acquisition structures, and repatriation of profits.

Intellectual Property

Trademark registration and enforcement, copyright protection, licensing negotiations, and IP litigation before courts and tribunals. Experience handling copyright infringement matters in contentious proceedings.

Mergers & Acquisitions

Advising acquirers and vendors on corporate transactions — due diligence, structure advice, sale and purchase documentation, regulatory approvals, and post-completion integration issues.

Real Estate & Property Law

Property transactions, lease documentation, landlord and tenant disputes, title investigations, and property-related litigation. Extensive experience handling rented premises disputes and property matters in Lahore.

Taxation

Corporate and individual tax planning, tax due diligence, withholding tax compliance, provincial revenue matters, and representation before tax tribunals. We have specific experience advising before the Punjab Revenue Authority.

Telecom & IT Law

Advising technology companies, telecoms operators, and digital businesses on regulatory compliance, licensing, data protection, and commercial contracts in Pakistan's rapidly evolving digital sector.

White-Collar Crime

One of the firm's core strengths. We defend individuals and corporates in NAB, FIA, and Anti-Corruption matters, and have been involved in some of Pakistan's most prominent public corruption proceedings. We also advise organisations on building anti-bribery compliance programmes.
